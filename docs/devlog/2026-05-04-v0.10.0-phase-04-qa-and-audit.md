# 2026-05-04 — Dashboard v2 Phase 04 (QA + Audit views)

## What shipped

**Phase 04 closes the dashboard-v2 plan.** Two new persona tabs land — **QA** (quality timeline, cost/quality scatter, commit-msg distribution, bug hotspots, rework root cause, intervention heatmap) and **Audit** (event stream + hash-chained compliance log with tamper-detect verify).

9 endpoints, 8 widgets, 5 sub-tabs across 2 personas — all wired through the existing persona-tab + hash-router architecture from Phase 01.

### Backend

- `internal/db/qa_queries.go` — 6 queries: `QualityTimeline`, `CostQualityScatter`, `CommitMsgDistribution` (fixed 4 buckets 0-25/25-50/50-75/75-100), `BugHotspots` (regression proxy: `lint_delta>0 OR tests_delta<0`), `ReworkCauses` (parses `task_attribution.session_outcomes` JSON, buckets test_fail/lint_fail/human_reject/timeout/other), `InterventionHeatmap` (engineer × hour-of-day, sums `human_intervention_count`).
- `internal/db/audit_queries.go` — `EventStream`, `AuditLog`, `VerifyAuditChain`, `AppendAuditEntry`. Hash chain: `curr_hash = sha256(prev_hash || actor || action || entity_type || entity_id || details || ts)` joined with `|`. Verify replays oldest-first, flags broken_at + reason on mismatch.
- `internal/server/qa_endpoints.go` — `/api/quality/{timeline,scatter,commit-msg}`, `/api/bug-hotspots`, `/api/rework/causes`, `/api/intervention/heatmap`.
- `internal/server/audit_endpoints.go` — `/api/events`, `/api/audit-log`, `/api/audit-log/verify` (GET + POST).
- `cmd/dashboard.go` registers both route packs.

### Frontend

- `index.html` — 2 new sidebar items (QA, Audit) and 2 `<section>` blocks with persona-pane sub-tabs (timeline / hotspots / rework, events / compliance).
- `style.css` — `.heat-0..heat-4` heat-cell ramp + `.audit-ok` / `.audit-fail` verify-badge styles.
- `app.js` — extends `applyHashPersona` / `togglePersonaVisibility` / `showPersonaSection` for `qa` + `audit`; adds 6 QA renderers (Chart.js line + scatter + horizontal-bar + doughnut, plus 2 heat tables) and 2 Audit renderers (event stream filter table, compliance log table with truncated 12-char hash + Verify Chain button).

## Schema

No migration. All queries hit existing tables (`runs`, `quality_metrics`, `task_attribution`, `events`, `audit_log`).
